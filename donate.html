Join Us To Eradicate Poverty

Poverty is a state or condition in which a person or community lacks the financial resources and essentials for a minimum
standard of living.Poverty means that the income level from employment is so low that basic human needs can't be met.
Poverty-stricken people and families might go without proper housing, clean water, healthy food, and medical attention.

Health

Increasing access to Medicaid for families may be one way to reduce poverty. Research shows that Medicaid helps pull families out
of poverty by providing access to affordable health coverage and lowering out-of-pocket costs.

Clean Water

Globally, 785 million people lack access to clean drinking water. The impacts of water scarcity affect families and their communities.
Without clean, easily accessible water, they can become locked in poverty for generations.

Medical

Try to give much medical help you can to people in poverty. Poverty is both a cause and a consequence of poor health in the world .
